Mahjong Aura - Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 23, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Mahjong Aura ("we", "our", "the app") collects, uses, and protects your information. By using the app, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

Mahjong Aura itself does not directly collect personal information. However, the app uses third-party advertising services that may collect certain data as described below.

Data Stored on Your Device

The following data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to external servers:

  • Game progress and saved states
  • Player statistics
  • Language preferences
  • Settings (such as game options)
  • Tutorial completion status

3. Third-Party Advertising

Mahjong Aura uses Google AdMob to display advertisements, including banner ads, interstitial ads, and rewarded video ads. Google AdMob may collect and process the following data:

  • Device Identifier (IDFA/Advertising ID): Used to serve personalized or non-personalized ads.
  • IP Address: Used for approximate location-based ad targeting.
  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, and screen size.
  • Ad Interaction Data: Information about ads viewed, clicked, or interacted with.

For more information about how Google collects and uses data, please visit: Google Privacy Policy and How Google uses information from apps.

4. GDPR Compliance (European Economic Area)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or the United Kingdom, a consent dialog will be shown when you first open the app. You can choose to:

  • Consent to personalized advertising
  • Choose non-personalized advertising
  • Manage your preferences at any time

Under GDPR, you have the right to access, rectify, delete, or restrict the processing of your personal data. For advertising-related data, you can manage your preferences through your device settings or by contacting Google directly.

5. App Tracking Transparency (iOS)

On iOS devices, the app will request your permission before accessing your device's advertising identifier (IDFA) in accordance with Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework. You can change this permission at any time in your device's Settings.

6. Children's Privacy

Mahjong Aura is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The ads displayed are not targeted at children.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties, except as required by our advertising partner (Google AdMob) as described in Section 3.

8. Data Security

All game data is stored locally on your device. We do not operate servers that store your personal information. Advertising-related data is handled by Google in accordance with their security practices.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
