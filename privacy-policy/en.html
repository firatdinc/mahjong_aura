Privacy Policy

Mahjong Aura

Last updated: March 5, 2026

1. Introduction

Mahjong Aura ("we", "our", or "the app") is a single-player Mahjong tile game. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. Mahjong Aura operates entirely offline and does not require an internet connection, account creation, or login.

3. Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Game progress and saved game state
  • Player statistics (games played, wins, losses, draws)
  • Language preference
  • Settings preferences (such as auto-draw)
  • Tutorial completion status

This data is stored using your device's local storage and is never transmitted to any server or third party.

4. Third-Party Services

Mahjong Aura does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not integrate any SDKs that collect user data.

5. Children's Privacy

Mahjong Aura does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The app is safe for users of all ages.

6. Data Sharing

Since we do not collect any data, we do not share, sell, or transfer any personal information to third parties.

7. Data Security

All game data is stored locally on your device. If you delete the app, all locally stored data will be permanently removed.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
